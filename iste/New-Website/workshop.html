Augmented Reality Workshop

Date Conducted :5th October, 2019.


On 5th October, 2019,. A workshop on AR was conducted by Team ISTE at B-110 , KJSCE. Augmented Reality is the integration of digital information with the user's environment in realtime. Unlike virtual reality which creates totally artificial environment, Augmented reality uses the existing environment and overlays new information on top of it. The session was conducted by 3 experts - Vishal,Rishabh And Ronit. Students were asked to install three softwares that were required(Blender,Unity and Android Studio). Students were able to grasp the concept easily. Good response was received from students.

Workshop on Component testing and identification

Date Conducted :8th September 2017

Time :5:15 pm


The first ISTE event for the year 2017-18 of the odd semester was a Workshop on Component testing and identification. It was conducted by Mr. Anil M. Purkar at KJ Somaiya Industrial Training Institute on 8th of September, held mainly for FEs and SEs to help them get accustomed to components such as resistors, capacitors, inductors, transistors, diodes, motors, digital ICs etc. It was an informative session and witnessed an active participation by the students. It commenced with an introduction to the basic components that the students will need to deal with during their labs. Further, they were also made aware of the applications of servo motors in automation industry and various other fields. Total number of students participated was 20.
